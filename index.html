    Features

    Dashboard

    You can control and customize the giveaways and all systems in the bot from the dashboard. All the features in the bot are updated simultaneously with the site and every move is instantly visible.

    Comfortable

    Since all systems are written and explained one by one, you can easily remember and perceive all the operations you have done. And you will not have any difficulties while performing these operations.

    Requirements

    There are requirements you can set when starting giveaways and add by editing users' participation in sweepstakes. For example: Inability to participate without having a specific role, not enough invites
